Hi! I'm Rebekah. I like to make things—often out of fabric, and sometimes out of code. Quilt Pattern Generator (QPG) is an attempt to combine the two: to use code to create random geometric quilt patterns out of squares and triangles. QPG provides yardage and pattern instructions for each quilt—if you find something you like, feel free to make it!

QPG is inspired by the amazing work of Libs Elliot, who creates and sells custom quilts and quilt patterns using Processing.
