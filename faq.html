Quilt and Block Size

QPG offers five quilt sizes and three finished block sizes, for a total of fifteen possible combinations. You can easily adjust the size of your quilt by adding sashing and borders or by removing rows or columns from the finished pattern.

Buying, Prepping, and Cutting Fabric

Each quilt pattern lists the necessary fabric yardage, rounded up to the nearest quarter of a yard. Directions for prepping and cutting the fabric are included in each pattern, but the quick version is: wash and dry your fabric before cutting, then use a rotary cutter and a self-healing cutting mat.
